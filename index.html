Frequently Asked Questions

Why am I not seeing new jokes?
This usually happens due to weak or unstable internet. Try refreshing the feed, switching to mobile data, or restarting the app.
Can I report inappropriate content?
Yes! Open the post and tap the Report button. Reports are reviewed within 24 hours to keep the app safe.
Why is the app taking time to load?
This can be due to poor connectivity, cached data, or temporary server load. Closing and reopening the app usually helps. Reinstalling the app clears corrupted cache and often fixes the issue completely.
